Our Programs

We are running three schools: Montessori pre-school (Kindergarten); First- and Sixth grade school; and Upper School and (JHS). Teachers, personnel and school directors collaborate together in order to have a solid curriculum that satisfies the expectations of each pupil, with special consideration provided to school-to-school transfers. We aim for smaller class sizes and outstanding teacher-student relationships and accomplish them.

ADMISSION

A package with a registration form, the parent/student handbook, the tuition and fee plan and payment options can be made available to prospective families at Glory Hill Academy. For each student (pupil), the following must be provided:

A completed form with all requested information on the up-to-date immunization records of the student (applicable to the Nursery Department) Current and previous school placements school records A current application fee passport photo

Upon completion of all required information, admission testing shall be arranged for an appointment. The test is necessary to help our school admission committee to determine the current level of skill, the level at which it is most likely to succeed within our school curriculum and, if registered, the potential requirement for special assistance services.

If the full range of information in the application package is being considered by the Admissions Committee, parents are requested to present the information at the request if it is additional information which can help evaluate the student's readiness to enrol in Glory Hill Academy.

Glory Hill Academy fails, in cultural, ethnic or racial terms, to discriminate against students or family members. However all classroom instruction is in the context of the doctrinal position of the school, which respects the religious preferences of parents and students. Students of other faiths shall not be excused from the school's religious instruction or activities. The school retains the right not to allow students who do not meet the academic requirements or who are unwilling to comply with the school Code of Conduct.

Intentionally, the class size is reduced so that teacher-student ratios are effective. In order to apply, eligible students will be admitted.

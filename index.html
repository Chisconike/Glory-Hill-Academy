A School of your choice

Glory Hill Academy is devoted to cultivating the souls, hearts and bodies of students in the whole human. However, we are primarily a school that strives tirelessly to preserve a reputation for academic excellence. The high expectations that we create are maintained by our unique program and dedicated faculty.

Our standards yield returns are what both our students and their parents desire. Students in every grade level consistently score higher than the national average.

Call To Action

We are running three schools: Montessori pre-school (Kindergarten); First- and Sixth grade school; and Upper School and (JHS). Teachers, personnel and school directors collaborate together in order to have a solid curriculum that satisfies the expectations of each pupil, with special consideration provided to school-to-school transfers. We aim for smaller class sizes and outstanding teacher-student relationships and accomplish them.
